About Us

One fateful evening, two friends found themselves trapped in the doldrums of boredom, endlessly searching for something—or someone—to shake up their routine. In that moment of longing for connection and excitement, they dreamed of an app where the bored could band together, sparking instant parties and impromptu gatherings.

At Bored, we understand that everyone faces the dull throb of tedium now and then. That's why we've crafted an oasis for the weary and the restless. With the Bored App, there's no reason to succumb to ennui—not anymore. Join us and transform your listless moments into legendary memories.
